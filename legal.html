Rates, yields, prices, rules, and fees can change at any time. The linked official source should be treated as the primary reference.

Rates, Yields, and Risk

Deposit products (savings/time deposits) generally use an annual interest rate. Many are tiered and/or promo based; multiple rows may exist to reflect tiers.

Non-deposit products (funds, bonds, dividends/stocks, metals) are not “guaranteed profit”. Returns can be negative, prices can change, and published figures may be historical snapshots.

Sources & Corrections
